Mizpah Masonic Youth Scholarship

Annual Scholarship Award

$1,000

Mizpah Lodge #302 awards a $1,000 scholarship to eligible applicants who are planning to further their post-secondary education during the following academic year. The scholarship is awarded directly to the academic institution.

Eligibility

  • Members in good standing of an Omaha Metropolitan Area DeMolay Chapter
  • Members in good standing of an Omaha Metropolitan Area Rainbow Assembly
  • Members in good standing of an Omaha Metropolitan Area Job's Daughter Bethel
  • Master Masons of Mizpah Lodge #302 under the age of 25
  • Applicants must be planning to further their post-secondary education

How to Apply

Applications must be postmarked by May 1 of each year.

Petition for Membership

Petition for Initiation

If you are interested in becoming a Freemason and joining Mizpah Lodge #302, the first step is to submit a Petition for Initiation. You must be a man of good moral character, at least 19 years of age, and believe in a Supreme Being.

We encourage you to visit our lodge, meet the brethren, and ask questions before petitioning. Feel free to contact us with any questions.
