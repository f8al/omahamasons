Club 1919

What is Club 1919?

Named in honor of the year Mizpah Lodge received its charter, Club 1919 is a voluntary charitable giving program. Members contribute a small recurring monthly amount — $19.19 per month via automatic PayPal debit — to support the lodge's community giving initiatives beyond the regular budget.

Not much more than the cost of a drive-thru, but pooled together it makes a meaningful difference.

How It Works

  • ✓ Sign up for automatic monthly PayPal debit
  • ✓ $19.19 is debited each month
  • ✓ 12-month commitment period
  • ✓ Funds pooled and distributed to local charities annually
