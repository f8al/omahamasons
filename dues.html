Lodge Dues

2026 annual dues information and payment options

2026 Annual Dues Breakdown

Item Amount
Mizpah Lodge Dues $115.50
Grand Lodge Per Capita Tax $37.00
Grand Lodge Assessments $3.00
Total Annual Dues $155.50

The per capita tax and assessments support the Nebraska Masonic Home, children's education programs, and youth organizations including DeMolay, Job's Daughters, and Rainbow for Girls.

Payment Methods

1

Grandview Portal

Sign into your account at ne.grandview.systems, locate Mizpah No. 302, click "Actions," and select "Pay Membership Dues."

Preferred method

2

Cash

Pay in person at any lodge meeting. Business meetings are held on the first Wednesday of the month. Contact the Secretary for arrangements.

3

Check

Make payable to Mizpah Lodge #302 and mail to:

PO Box 6362
Omaha, NE 68106-0362
