Club 1919

Small donations, big impact for our community

What is Club 1919?

Club 1919 is named in honor of the year Mizpah Lodge #302 received its charter. It is a voluntary charitable giving program where members contribute a small, recurring monthly amount to support the lodge's community giving initiatives beyond the regular lodge budget.

Monthly Contribution

$19.19
per month · automatic PayPal debit

Not much more than the cost of going through a drive-thru, but it adds up over time.

How It Works

  • ✓ Sign up for automatic monthly PayPal debit
  • ✓ $19.19 is debited each month
  • ✓ 12-month commitment period
  • ✓ Funds are pooled and distributed to local charities

Where the Money Goes

All Club 1919 funds are set aside separately from standard lodge operations. The collected funds are pooled together and distributed annually to local charitable causes, supplementing the lodge's existing charitable programs.

Through small, regular donations, we collectively make a meaningful difference in our community.
